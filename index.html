Lucas Hainsworth

Lucas (Luc) has almost 10 years of experience in Financial Services, 7+ of this at a major Swiss Bank. He founded Erebus Financial Planning & Wealth Management in response to how he felt the industry treated its customers in Australia.

What We Do

Our Mission

"To help our clients identify their surplus income, pay off their debts, right size their superannuation fund, reduce portfolio frictions, implement an appropriate asset allocation and risk management strategy and provide ongoing coaching and support to ensure that discipline is maintained"

Our Method

1. Control Cashflow
2. Educate "What Finance is all about"
3. Understand and explain Financial Market History
4. Teach people how to overcome themselves

OUR METHOD

1. Determine Why you came to see us
2. What are we going to do about it?
3. Prepare advice and ensure it meets your identified goal
4. Implement it

OUR SALES TARGETS

We have no sales targets, no sales meetings, no products that we are required to sell.
We only provide answers and guidance as to how to organise your affairs and assist you with implementation.
